# Groups

A group in Thunder is a named collection of users. Use groups to organize users and assign roles to multiple users at once rather than assigning roles individually.

Groups belong to an **organization unit** and can contain both users and other groups as members, allowing you to build hierarchical groupings when needed.


## Create a Group

1. Navigate to **Groups** in the Thunder Console.
2. Click **New Group**.
3. Enter a **Name** and an optional **Description** for the group.
4. Select the **Organization Unit** the group belongs to.
5. Optionally, add **Members** — search for users or existing groups to include.
6. Click **Create Group**.


## View and Search Groups

Navigate to **Groups** in the Thunder Console to see all groups. Use the search bar to find groups by name.

Click on any group to view its details, description, organization unit, and current members.


## Update a Group

1. Open the group from the **Groups** list.
2. Edit the **Name** or **Description** as needed.
3. Click **Save**.

:::note
Changing a group's organization unit is not supported after creation. Delete and recreate the group under the correct organization unit if needed.
:::


## Delete a Group

1. Open the group from the **Groups** list.
2. Click **Delete** and confirm.

:::warning
Deleting a group is permanent. Members are not deleted — only the group itself and its role assignments are removed.
:::


## Manage Members

### Add Members

1. Open the group from the **Groups** list.
2. Click **Add Members**.
3. Search for the users or groups you want to add.
4. Select the members and click **Add**.

Members can be **users** or other **groups**. Adding a group as a member includes all users in that group transitively.

### Remove Members

1. Open the group from the **Groups** list.
2. Locate the member you want to remove.
3. Click **Remove** next to the member and confirm.


## Assign Roles to a Group

Roles assigned to a group apply to all members of that group. This is the recommended way to grant permissions to a set of users.

To assign a role to a group, see [Roles](/guides/guides/roles).
